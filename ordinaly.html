Three Columns with Header and Footers

You see a part of an ordinal tree with some recursive countable ordinals. Each column contains a sequence converging to the ordinal at the bottom. The current column is the middle one, on the right, you see the sequence between the two consecutive ordinals selected in the middle column (unless they are immediate successors). Using keyboard arrows, you can move up / down in the middle column, or to jump to another column (left = outside, right = expand).
